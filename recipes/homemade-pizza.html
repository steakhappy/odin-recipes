Lasagna

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish of Italian origin, characterized by a flatbread base topped with various savory ingredients. The classic Margherita features a tomato sauce base, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, but countless variations exist, from simple marinara pizzas to those adorned with meats, vegetables, and a wide array of other toppings. Pizza's versatility and adaptability, along with its portability and delicious taste, have made it a popular and versatile food choice worldwide.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For the Pizza:

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
