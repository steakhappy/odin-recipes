Francesinha

Description

Francesinha is a Portuguese sandwich, originating from Porto, known for its layers of bread, various meats (like ham, sausage, and steak), cheese, and a rich, spicy tomato and beer sauce.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Steak
  • Sausages
  • Cheese
  • Fried Egg
  • Tomato and Beer-based sauce

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce
  2. Cook the Meats
  3. Assemble The Sandwich
  4. Melt The Cheese
  5. Pour The Sauce
