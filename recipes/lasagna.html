Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian pasta dish characterized by its layered structure of wide, flat noodles stacked with various fillings like meat sauce, béchamel, vegetables, and cheese, all baked together. The dish typically involves alternate layers of pasta and fillings, creating a rich and flavorful casserole-like texture. While traditional lasagna uses a meat-based ragù sauce and béchamel, modern variations include vegetarian options, vegetable fillings, and different cheese combinations.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
